Return Policy

At Automart, we strive to provide our customers with a seamless shopping experience. We understand that occasionally, you may need to return a product, and we have established a comprehensive return policy to ensure your satisfaction. Please review the following guidelines regarding our return process:

  1. Eligibility:
    • Products must be purchased directly from Automart.
    • The product must be in its original condition, unused, and undamaged.
    • Returns must be initiated within 7 days of the purchase date.
    • Proof of purchase, such as an order number or receipt, is required.
  2. Return Process:
    • Contact our customer support team to initiate the return process. They will provide you with instructions and a return authorization (RA) number.
    • Pack the product securely in its original packaging to avoid damage during transit. Include all accessories, manuals, and documentation.
    • Clearly write the provided RA number on the package.
    • Ship the package back to our designated return address, using a trackable shipping method. The return shipping cost is the responsibility of the customer, unless the return is due to a defective or incorrect item.
  3. Return Options:
    • Refund: Upon receiving and inspecting the returned product, we will issue a refund to your original payment method. Please allow [X] business days for the refund to be processed and reflected in your account.
    • Store Credit: If you prefer, we can provide a store credit equivalent to the value of the returned product, which you can use for future purchases.
  4. Non-Returnable Items:
    • Certain items are non-returnable for hygiene or safety reasons, such as opened software, downloaded content, or personalized items. Please check the product description or contact our customer support for more information.
  5. Damaged or Defective Items:
    • If you receive a damaged or defective product, please notify our customer support immediately. We may request supporting evidence, such as photos or videos, to assess the issue. In such cases, we will arrange for a replacement or provide a prepaid shipping label for the return.
  6. Exchanges:
    • We currently do not offer direct exchanges. To exchange a product, please follow the return process to receive a refund or store credit. You can then place a new order for the desired item.
  7. Cancellations:
    • If you need to cancel an order, please contact us as soon as possible. If the order has not yet been shipped, we will cancel it and issue a refund.
  8. Policy Updates:
    • We reserve the right to modify or update our return policy at any time. The most current version will be posted on our website.
