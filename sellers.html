Sellers Policy

At Automart, we value our partnership with sellers who choose to list their car hardware products on our platform. This Sellers Policy outlines the guidelines and terms that govern the selling process on Automart. By becoming a seller on our platform, you agree to comply with the following policies:

  1. Product Listing:
    • Sellers are responsible for accurately describing their products, including specifications, features, condition, and pricing.
    • All listed products must be in compliance with relevant laws and regulations.
    • Sellers should provide clear and high-quality product images to enhance the listing.
  2. Order Fulfillment:
    • Sellers are responsible for promptly processing and fulfilling customer orders.
    • Products should be packaged securely to ensure safe delivery to customers.
    • Sellers must provide accurate shipment tracking information to customers and update the order status accordingly.
  3. Pricing and Payment:
    • Sellers are responsible for setting competitive and reasonable prices for their products.
    • Payment for sales, including applicable taxes and fees, will be facilitated through the Automart platform.
    • Sellers will receive payment for completed and delivered orders, minus any applicable transaction fees or commission charges.
  4. Product Warranty and Returns:
    • Sellers should clearly specify the warranty terms, if any, for their products.
    • Sellers are responsible for handling returns and refunds according to Automart's Return Policy.
    • It is recommended that sellers promptly address customer concerns and provide timely support and resolution.
  5. Prohibited Items:
    • Sellers must not list or sell any prohibited items, including counterfeit products, stolen goods, or items that infringe upon intellectual property rights.
    • Sellers must comply with all relevant laws and regulations pertaining to the sale of their products.
  6. Communication and Customer Support:
    • Sellers are expected to maintain prompt and professional communication with customers regarding their listings, orders, and any inquiries or concerns.
    • Timely response and assistance in resolving customer issues are essential for a positive selling experience.
  7. Policy Updates:
    • We reserve the right to modify or update our Buyers Policy at any time. The most current version will be posted on our website.
